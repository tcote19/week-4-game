Crystals Collector

Built by Thomas Cote

You will be shown a random number.

There are four crystals. You must click a crystal to add a specific number to your total.

Win the game by matching your total to the random number shown at the start of the game.

You will lose the game if your total goes over the random number.

The value of each crystal will be hidden until clicked. Each time the game starts, a new value will be assigned to the crystals.
